About Me

I graduated in May of 2018 from The Johns Hopkins University (JHU) with a Bachelor's Degree in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering. After graduating I began to work as a Research Engineer at the JHU Applied Physics Laboratory (APL) at the Intelligent Systems Center (ISC). I am actively persuing a Masters degree in Computer Science at JHU while working full-time at the lab. From the ISC I works on projects managing Big Data cloud infrastructure and developing Machine Learning algorithms to process it. I also work as a Flight Software Engineer on NASA's Double Asteroid Redirection Task (DART) and am a member of the Planetary Society.

I created Spacewired to share the passion I have for space, and to share with you all the cool things going on in the space industry, planetary science and engineering.
